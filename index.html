The Science of Smog

Inversion: Hot air from the sun usually lifts smog away. But in Bishkek winters, cold mountain air creates a heavy lid that traps poisons at our eye level.

PM2.5: These particles are so small (2.5 microns) that your body cannot filter them out. They cause long-term damage to the heart and brain.

Green Lungs: Trees don't just 'make air', they act as massive sponges that physically catch coal dust on their leaves.
